## Labels

You need a type of bug:

- `bug::possible`
- `bug::confirmed`
- `bug::regression`
- `bug::upstream`

A severity and a priority. See [here](https://gitlab.com/reductech/kb/-/wikis/Development/Labels#bugs-c85a16).
